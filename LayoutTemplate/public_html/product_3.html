DeWalt 4 Pack Trigger Clamps

DEWALT is the number one brand for woodworking and the Pro. These DEWALT 4-Pack Medium and Large Trigger Clamp pack is ideal for a variety of jobs including holding, spreading and glue ups. The Large Trigger Clamps have the capability to convert to a spreader with one touch of the quick change button.
