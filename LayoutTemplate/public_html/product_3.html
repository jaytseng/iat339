DeWalt 4 PACK TRIGGER CLAMPS

DEWALT is the number one brand for woodworking and the Pro. These DEWALT 4-Pack Medium and Large Trigger Clamp pack is ideal for a variety of jobs including holding, spreading and glue ups.
